Dynasty Appreciation Engine

Core Scores

Talent Score: Raw player skill based on production, underlying skills, and projection.

Stability Multiplier: Role security, sample size, health, command/contact stability, and development risk.

League Fit Modifier: Adjusts for our 10-team Fantrax H2H points league.

DVS: Dynasty Value Score = Talent × Stability × League Fit.

League Fit Priorities

  • Hitters: power, walks, OBP, extra-base hits, playing time.
  • Pitchers: strikeouts, innings, starts, rotation security, deep-game upside.
  • Discount: speed-only hitters, empty batting average, low-strikeout pitchers, streamer-only arms.
