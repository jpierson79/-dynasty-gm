1. Import HarryKnowsBall CSV

Imports/updates player names, positions, teams, ages, and values. Existing owner/scouting data is preserved.

2. Import Fantrax All Players CSV

Use the export that includes every player. The app reads the Status column as the owner/source of truth. Blank/FA/Waivers becomes FREE AGENT.

Import Tips

Best weekly rhythm: export full HKB list, export Fantrax all players with Status, import both. The app merges values and ownership automatically.

Statcast Engine

Upload Baseball Savant hitter or pitcher CSV exports directly. Native Statcast headers are detected automatically and matched to existing players by player ID or name.

Supported Metrics

Statcast hitters: last_name, first_name, player_id, player_age, xba, xslg, xwoba, xobp, xiso, exit_velocity_avg, launch_angle_avg, sweet_spot_percent, barrel_batted_rate, hard_hit_percent, whiff_percent, k_percent, bb_percent, avg_swing_speed, fast_swing_rate, attack_angle, attack_direction, ideal_angle_rate, vertical_swing_path.

Legacy advanced CSVs still work: hitter and pitcher metrics from the prior Advanced Stats import are still supported.

How Scores Work

The Player Engine combines HKB value, league scoring, roster context, Statcast or advanced metrics, manager preferences, trade history, roster pressure, and portfolio fit into reusable cached player scores.

  • Dynasty Asset Score weighs long-term player quality, appreciation, scarcity, liquidity, and risk.
  • Championship Impact favors players who help the current competitive window.
  • Roster Pressure highlights core holds, trade chips, watchlist players, and cut candidates.
  • Market Edge and Trade Center recommendations use cached player scores plus ownership, manager fit, and league demand.
